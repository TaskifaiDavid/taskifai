# TaskifAI Pricing Page – Recommended Changes (Consultant Notes)

## 🎯 Goal
- Lower upfront friction for new customers (especially first 3–5 “founding customers”).
- Shift value capture from heavy setup fees → recurring revenue (monthly/annual).
- Secure **12-month commitments** with discounts and value stacking.
- Keep TaskifAI positioned as a **premium, enterprise-grade solution**.

---

## 🔧 Key Adjustments

### 1. Setup Fees
- Current: €15k / €30k / €50k (too high for early adoption).
- New:  
  - Starter: €7,500 (Founding Customers: €5,000)  
  - Growth: €15,000 (Founding Customers: €10,000)  
  - Enterprise: €25,000 (Founding Customers: €17,500)  

👉 Keeps commitment serious (covers dev/migration work) but lowers sticker shock.

---

### 2. Monthly vs Annual Pricing (Introduce Toggle)
- Add a **Monthly / Annual toggle** (annual shown as default).
- Structure:  
  - **Monthly (cancel anytime)**: baseline price.  
  - **Annual (paid monthly)**: ~15% cheaper.  
  - **Annual (prepaid)**: ~20% cheaper (2–3 months free).  

#### Example (Starter Tier, 1–10 resellers)
- Setup: €7,500 (Founding €5,000)  
- Monthly: €1,250/month  
- Annual (paid monthly): €1,100/month  
- Annual (prepaid): €12,000/year  

---

### 3. Value Stacking for Annual Plans
Include extras **only for annual subscribers**:
- 1 extra custom dashboard
- Priority onboarding & support SLA
- Dedicated training session
- Price lock guarantee for 2 years

👉 Makes annual feel premium, not just “cheaper.”

---

### 4. Presentation Changes on Page
- Replace **“Starting from €15,000”** with **“Setup: from €7,500”**.  
- Place **monthly/annual pricing front and center**, with annual highlighted as **Most Popular**.  
- Keep the **“Save 20–30 hours/month”** benefit, but add a **“Save 15% with annual”** badge.  
- Add a **“Founding Customer Offer”** note (valid for limited clients).  

---

**Starter** (1–10 resellers)

---

## 📌 Summary
- **Lower setup fees** to reduce entry barrier.  
- **Shift value to recurring revenue** with higher monthly.  
- **Annual default** with discounts + extras.  
- **Founding Customer offer** to secure first deals quickly while still profitable.  

This balance gives you **cash now** (via setup + prepay) and **recurring runway** (monthly/annual contracts), while keeping TaskifAI positioned as a premium B2B SaaS solution.
